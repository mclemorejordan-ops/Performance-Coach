Performance Coach Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

Performance Coach is a fitness tracking app for routines, workout logs, progress, protein tracking, weight tracking, and optional social features. This policy explains what information the app handles and how that information is used.

Information We Handle

  • Account information such as your email address and authentication session.
  • Profile information such as your name, username, bio, and app preferences.
  • Fitness data you enter, including routines, workout logs, set data, attendance, weight logs, protein logs, and goals.
  • Social data, if you use social features, such as follows, activity events, likes, comments, notifications, and challenges.
  • Optional outdoor cardio tracking data such as distance, elapsed time, pace, location-derived movement, and motion activity while a tracked session is active.
  • Optional HealthKit workout write data when you choose to save a tracked outdoor cardio session to Apple Health.

How Information Is Stored

  • Performance Coach stores app state locally on your device so the app can load your data quickly and support local snapshots and backup export/import.
  • When you use an account, supported account, profile, routine, workout, and social data may also be stored in Supabase-backed cloud services used by the app.
  • Apple Health data is only written when you complete a tracked outdoor cardio session and choose to save that session through the app flow.

How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • To sync your routines, workout history, and profile data across app sessions.
  • To power progress views, attendance history, nutrition tracking, and social features.
  • To calculate tracked outdoor cardio metrics such as distance and pace.
  • To write completed tracked outdoor cardio workouts to Apple Health if you grant HealthKit permission.

Permissions

  • Location: used during active outdoor cardio tracking to estimate route movement, distance, and pace.
  • Motion Activity: used during active outdoor cardio tracking to improve movement detection and reduce GPS drift.
  • HealthKit: used to write completed tracked outdoor cardio workouts to Apple Health.

Backup, Export, and Import

  • The app supports exporting your local app data as a backup file.
  • The app supports importing a previously exported backup file.
  • Local rotating snapshots may also be stored on-device to support restore of recent local states.

Account Deletion

If you created an account in Performance Coach, you can request deletion in the app from the Settings support section. Deleting your account is intended to permanently delete your account and associated cloud data handled by the app.

Data Sharing

Performance Coach uses Supabase infrastructure to support authentication and app-backed cloud data storage. The app does not sell your personal information.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support information on the Support page.
